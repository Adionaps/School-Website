Facilities

Hostel
Optional Boarding Facility for boys of Class I to XII

Schools boarding house is located within the clean and green environment on the school campus. It is like a home away from home. All 4 meal facility (including breakfast, lunch, evening snacks and dinner) is available for the students alongwith other facilities like Extra Coaching, Recreation, Additional Sports Activities, outing such as movie, picnics etc. for the convenience and safety of the boarding house students house masters/mothers, house keeping staff, nurse, drivers, guards are available round the clock 24X7 on campus.

Transport
Safe Transport

The school provides a safe and convenient transport facility as the safety and security of our students is our priority. The speed governors are installed in each bus and the buses are all CCTV and GPS equipped.
